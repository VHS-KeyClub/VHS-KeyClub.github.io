ABOUT VOORHEESVILLE KEY CLUB

We are an organization based in Clayton A. Bouton High School. Our mission is to spread the values of sevice, leadership, and caring to our community.

International

Key Club is the oldest and largest service program for high school students. Key Club has approximately 270,000 members in approximately 5,000 clubs. Key Club is represented in 37 countries.

State

Voorheesville Key Club is a part of division 16 in the district of New York. We partner with the nine other schools in our division to carry out our service projects.

Local

We are partnered with New Scotland Kiwanis in our goal to make our own village a better place. In the past we have done this by raising money for Kamp Kiwanis and collecting items for the New Scotland Food Pantry.
